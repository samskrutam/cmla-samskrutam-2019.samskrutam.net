## संस्कृतिः

### Lord Ganesha

Teach about Ganesha being the Lord of Obstacles. By praying to him, the obstacles in our tasks will be removed.
Ganesha lives in Kailasa with his parents, Lord Shiva and Parvathi His brother is Lord Karthikeya
He has a vehicle – MushakaH ( मूषकः )
The sloka we learnt is related to Ganesha
Practice parts of the body with Ganesha’s image
Teach the story of Ganesha – Narada – Fruit – Doing namaskaras to his parents to win the contest

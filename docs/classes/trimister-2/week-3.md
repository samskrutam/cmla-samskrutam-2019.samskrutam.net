## पठनम्
अः to औः ( Concept of विसर्गः with  स्वरः )

Use the file: [अः to औः.pdf](/resources/trimister-2/week-3/अः - औः.pdf) to show the स्वराः with  विसर्गः | विसर्गः is the sound we make when the wind comes out of the mouth from the कण्ठः । This sound will be similar to ह, provided we add the vowel sound अ to it. But this is not equal to ह.

The विसर्ग-युक्त-स्वरः pronunciations are bit different from one another. Hence the [visarga.mp3](/resources/trimister-2/week-3/visarga.mp3) file is also provided. Please listen to the sounds carefully and teach the sounds to the students.

## लेखनम्

अः to औः ( Concept of विसर्गः with स्वरः )

Make the students write the sounds ( विसर्ग-युक्त-स्वरः )when the teacher says the sound. Also say the sounds with विसर्ग-युक्त-स्वरः at the end and see if the students can recognize the sound.

Ex: रामः, रामाः, भूमिः , मतिः,वधूः, शम्भुः, मातः , हरेः , शुनकैः, धेन्वॊः
